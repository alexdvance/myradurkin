About

I am a licensed clinical social worker with a practice in Davis Square, Somerville. I provide psychotherapy and couples counseling to individuals and couples, adolescents and adults. I specialize in sex therapy. In addition, I work with eating disorders, and with performance anxiety and creativity issues in the arts, in athletics and in the workplace. I use traditional psychotherapeutic tools, as well as self-hypnosis and mindfulness meditation.

Experience

I have maintained a private practice for more than 25 years, and have worked as well in community mental health and hospital settings, and in colleges and conservatories. My background as a performing musician has given me a deep interest in performance and creativity issues; I founded the Mind/Body Program at the Longy School of Music in Cambridge, and am a national consultant to colleges and conservatories on performance issues. Other areas of expertise include sex therapy, eating disorders, and self-directed hypnotherapy.

About

I provide psychotherapy and couples counseling to individuals and couples, adolescents and adults. I specialize in sex therapy. In addition, I work with eating disorders, and with performance anxiety and creativity issues in the arts, in athletics and in the workplace. I use traditional psychotherapeutic tools, as well as self-hypnosis and mindfulness meditation.

Experience

I have maintained a private practice for more than 30 years, and have worked in community mental health, hospitals, colleges and conservatories. My areas of expertise are sex therapy, eating disorders, performance issues, and self-directed hypnotherapy. My background as a performing musician has given me a deep interest in performance and creativity issues; I founded the Mind/Body Program at the Longy School of Music in Cambridge, and am a national consultant to colleges and conservatories on performance issues.
